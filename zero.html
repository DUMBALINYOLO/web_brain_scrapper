How to take a tour:

Choose from the Tour descriptions on the K-web map.

You’ll see the first person or thing on the Tour.

The link to the next person or thing on the Tour will be highlighted.

Mouse over a name to see who or what it is

Mouse over the links to see the connections

Click on the next person or thing.

Read all about them.

Travel onward, till you get to the end of the Mystery Tour!

Mystery tours begin and end with familiar but fun concepts, but everything in the Knowledge Web connects to everything else.

If you want to leave the Tour at any time, mouse over the name you’re interested in.

If you like what you see, click on that name.  Filaments tell you how they are related. Green is for financial , orange is Mystery Tour , purple is influence , red is for fights .
